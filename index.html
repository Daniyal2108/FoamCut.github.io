About Foam Cuts

Our purpose built manufacturing facility and retail store in Bilston, West Midlands.

In 2000, we set up Foam Cuts as a source for the world to find bespoke foam blocks and pieces. Our buyers are incredibly diverse – from the master upholsters to the yoga practitioner to the furniture manufacturer to the small-time crafter; each person seeking the perfect piece to suit their needs.

As the largest online supplier of foam in the UK, we sell to tens of thousands of customers every year. Our purpose-built facility in Bilston means we can produce whatever you need and fabricate polyurethane, polyethylene and other types of foam. We can also cut the foam to be any shape or size that you need.

With over 20 years of experience, the management team of Foam Cuts have substantial experience at converting foam, using foam and sharing foam knowledge.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

We strongly recommend the use of all over polyfibre (dacron) wrap and stockinette undercover for all upholstery foam. It prolongs the life of the foam, protects the edges and helps prevent permanent creasing of the outer cover. When selecting polyfibre you will also get FREE stockinette undercover. When measuring for cushions, it is NOT necessary to make any adjustments for polyfibre wraps.
