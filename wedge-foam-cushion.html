Measuring Guide:

We call "width" the side view or left to right measurement. In a foam seat cushion this is the dimension behind your knees. "Length" is the front to back measurement.

Don't measure the old foam. Old foam is usually deformed or collapsed with age.

For replacing cushion fillings, always measure between the seams or between the cording of the cover with the filling inside, then add 1" (1.5-2.5cm) to the thickness depending on how much extra fullness you want in your cushion.

If you are adding a fibre wrap, please do not allow for this in your measurement as the fibre wrap will compress and fill your cushion out nicely.
